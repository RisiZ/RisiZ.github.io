Creating a game throughout different platforms to relieve stress and give a message of hope

Project Goal

To create an experience that enables the player to be curious, remove them from their daily lives and give a message of hope and how curiosity can be a positive effect

Project Challanges

  • • Allocating time to learning and then developing
  • • Understanding teams strengths & weaknesses
  • • Motivating players to continue playing
  • • Developing a narrative that is both complex and simple

A wizard ( Zeus) created you out of the darkness built with curiosity and has given you a box filled with mystery. You and a key are given as a gift to a witch (Epithelium) as a familiar. The witch who holds the key to the box instructs you to not take her key and open it. As the curious witch's familiar, you want to know what’s in the box - no matter what the cost is. Will you head the witch’s warnings or will you be curious enough to see what’s in the box is a reward or if it’s curse?
