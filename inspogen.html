Inspired by my passion for writing and digital painting

My Personal Experience

As a creative writer & digital painter, I'd fall into times where I need quick inspiration or a decision to be made to keep going on forward, instead of spending a lot of time dwelling on one decision. I didn't want to create generators that cheated the process of creating either. I'm currently looking to fully develop the website (except a more modified design). This is based off my first use of javascript project Prompt Generator

Problem Statement

Writers and artists need quick inspiration or decision to continue their work, rather than losing valuable time. Current generator/inspiration websites either have only words, or pictures. Navigation is confusing, too dated or missing aspects that is normally. In addition, they tend to have too little information or not enough



Project Goal

Inspiration Generator’s goal is to cater to three different users (hobbyists, teachers, professionals) who are story creators. The website will allow a level of personalization so that the user may receive the best options. The website is consistent of user friendly navigation, providing the user to choose the level of customization, inspiring and quality imagery

Features

  • Quick or Advanced

    The first generators in each page is the quick version. A person who'se looking to do something quick is going to stay on the site for a shorter amount of time and give up quickly. However, someone who'se looking for in more depth will stay longer. Thus, advanced is placed second. It has a lot of details, covering different aspects of a story or design.

  • Word & Picture Generators

    Most generators just have either one or the other. Images spark inspirations as well. It was important to include it, while also sourcing images from different websites that are free to use.

  • Save/Copy

    The ability to save/copy or even download what was generated for later onto their own computer.
