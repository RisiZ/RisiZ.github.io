Inspo Generator

A case study for developing a inspiration generator website for artists and writers.
The case covers the goal, navigation, small sample of user testing, competitors and design

View Mockup

Skills Used: Card sorting/user testing, navigation, UI & UX design


  • PROCESS

Problem Statement

Writers and artists all run into a block or need to make a quick decision in the process of developing their work; causing them to waste valuable time. Current similar generators lack in fast navigation, lack in visual design and imagery, don’t offer user’s an option for their input, and often don’t balance the amount of detail given to the user of the prompt/characteristic/inspiration. Providing very little customization can lead to frustration. An overwhelming amount of choices can increase the amount of wasted time rather than decreasing. Prompt generators don’t often include imagery. Imagery can make a bigger impact than words.

Competitors

Competitors such as seventhsanctum & masterpiece-generator lacked heavily in user friendly experience because of their difficult navigation and in some cases mislabeling of links.Their designs were dated, in some cases, gave information anxiety. One competitor didn't have a search bar and the other didn't seem to have a home page. Yet, they're the most used generators.

Navigation

When developing the navigation I thought through the main aspects of a story: Characters, Plot, Scene. I also based it off an assignment from grade school, pulling out one item from each category (Character, Location & item). I thought about all aspects I'd like in a generator (as a writer) and organized it into sections. There's also the frustration of not being able to quickly generate one item and go. Therefor, at the top of each generator there is a quick generator and then an option for a detailed one.

Project Goal

Inspiration Generator’s goal is to cater to three different users (hobbyists, teachers, professionals) who are story creators. The website will allow a level of personalization so that the user may receive the best options. The website is consistent of user friendly navigation, providing the user to choose the level of customization, inspiring and quality imagery and allows users to input their suggestions and allow them to create their own generators. This all allows the user to save overall time and become inspired when they are at writer’s block.

Target Audience

The target audience is users whom are over the age of fourteen who are either hobbyists (writers/artists), professionals or teachers who may use it as assignment.

User Testing

The first method was card sorting. Through card sorting, it taught that users associated words differently than imaged. Some associated location with a time period, another a country and so on. The user testing of a website was prior to the development of designing the website. It was based off one of the current generators: seventhsanctum, to grab understanding of what users needed and how long they took to complete a task. to create my own.

Design & Inspiration

The focus was to create an inspiring, earthy and serene, modern design. That resulted to using a fairly pastel color palette, with a green blue being the main color because of it's association with creativity. I also focused on certain colors causing different actions (for buttons). I was inspired by a previous project I did making a working prompt generator (I've always been fascinated by generators) using Html, Css and Javascript.
