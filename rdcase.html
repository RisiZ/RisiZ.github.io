Story, goal & requirements

Family member accounts please!

The story

Conner ( Staff ) wants to call Audrey( Patient ) to ask to rebook her at a different time and provider. She agrees, In the meantime, Audrey(Patient) asks to have her daughter Cassandra to be added to her account.

The goal

Add a patient's family member to an existing account.

Requirements

- Add existing patients to an existing account as a family member
- Patients create profiles for family members to add to their account
- Patients not able to add existing accounts to their profile on their own
- Staff can add an existing account to another account
- Verification email must be sent to the existing account that is being added to another account
- Must be able to have a separate login for a family member
- Allow to separate account from primary account holder in the event of turning 18+

Persona Summary

The users

Conner Baskerr

Staff member

34

Bio
Conner has worked at RD for 3 years. He's always comes off as a friendly and caring person.

Pain Points

  • Providing a fast experience
  • Fast information
  • Addressing patients correctly

Frustrations

  • Information not place where it's most efficient to find.
  • Info missing: age, file associated with what appointment
  • Difficulty pronouncing names and understanding how a patient wants to be addressed

"Being able to provide and recieve information quickly is very important"

Audrey Books

Patient

42

Bio
Addy is a photographer and a mom of two and is always on the go.

Pain Points

  • Creating separate accounts
  • Being addressed correctly
  • Control of modifying accounts

Frustrations

  • Managing separate accounts per family member & remembering passwords
  • Does not go by "Audrey" due to past negative association. Would prefer "Addy"
  • Having to go back and forth between two or more accounts

"It's tedious to go back and forth between accounts. Why can't I just have one?"

Cassandra Books

Family Member

Bio
Cass is incrediably smart and she tends to still be a shy person.

Pain Points

  • Booking appointments
  • Talking on the phone
  • Seeing inforamtion

Frustrations

  • Booking appointments on her own.
  • Not being notified on her own email when her appointments is at
  • No access to her own account because her mom manages it

"I want my account but I don't want to book appointments"

Userflow

Add family member

When creating this userflow, I took in consideration of having to include two different routes: a user who already has multiple accounts for family members, plus how we will merge their accounts securely and a user who is entirely new.

UI Decisions

Blues & Contrast

#F4F7FF
#7A85A1
#182137
#467CFF
#FAFBFF
#FFFFFF

Why blue & monochormes?. Blue is often used in the pharmarcy & medicine industry. For this decision, I've decided to remain within that mold. In virtual health care, it's so important to gain trust, as it's still new. With a more professional apperance, yet still friendliness with the light colors representing a blue sky and roundness.

My font choice is geometric as it geometric fonts are known to appear more friendly. I chose bigger font especially for mobile as multitude of users at various of ages and needs will be using it.
