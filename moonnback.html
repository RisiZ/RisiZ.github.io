In 2021, long-distance has become the norm

Problem Statement

How may we help long-distance couples connect and create emotional intimacy by spending quality time online in one cohesive space?

Scope

24 Hours, User research,
3 pro reviews & one
wholesome project

Moon & Back was made with myself & another amazing student in 24 hours as a response to a Protothon challenge. We worked collaboratively together in each phase. Our personal deliverables includes: User interviews & Surveys, user journey, prototyping main features and a wholesome flirty presentation to suit Valentines Day . Our goal was to have grounded user research supporting the project, have 3 professional designers in the field provide critique, high fidelity prototype. We personally reached out other UX/UI professionals that work at Facebook to make this project the best it can be.

We identified these user needs:

Provide long distance couples privacy & their own personal space to define their own intimacy

Motivate long-distance couples to connect and resolve their miscommunication

Our Solution

A swiss army knife for long distance relationships to chat, archive your memories , play games and level up your love in from of a mobile app

Persona & user journey

Simon & Thomas are in 1 year long-distance relationship. Thomas is mad at Simon because he can't meet up as promised on Valentine's day.


Bio


Simon and Thomas met at a bar in New York, now they have a stable relationship. Simon is a bartender working in New York and Thomas is a photographer working in Los Angeles. Even though they are best friends in each other’s life they still bickers about smell problems happened in their life due to long distance relationship. They hope to find a App that helps them to grow closer and build deeper connection together.


Scenario


It’s Balentines, Thomas promised he would fly to Simon’s city to spend valentine together, but he suddenly got a emergency commission come up so he couldn’t make it. This situation made Thomas extremely upset.


Goals


• Show each other support
• Reschedule Dates
• Have fun together online
• Share couple’s activity
• Increase communication
• Insecurity

Reflect

The difficulties we faced was deciding which problem we'd like to focus on solving. We realized that one of main issues was having one spot to communicate and not be scattered between multiple platforms. We received a lot of critiques on how may we present this application and how our solution was fairing. Our application worked to solve a list of needs that would need to be developed in detail. Overall, it showed the benefits of working with an individual with the same energy and passion matched with my own.
