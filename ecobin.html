Project Challanges

24 Hour Hack

4 individuals with different fields of study whom never met collaborating

  • • Communicating design, the site map, user experience
  • • Taking in account all the different perspectives
  • • Listening, understanding, learning from Teammates
  • • Adapting & organizing workflows
  • • Spending some team bonding time
  • • Managing time & coming up with a solution with problems that put our project at risk

In some communities Educating Youth on Recycling has been reduced or cancelled because of taxes.

Are you confused on which bins you place your garbage? I was, and my frustration led me to find a solution .

Pain Points

  • • Accessibility to sort the garbage
  • • Spending more time then needed
  • • Motivation to build a habit

Other Factors

  • • Easy to adopt for other businesses
  • • Educates users with persistent use
  • • Usable for a wide audience

Solution

Solution Overview

A mobile app that sorts garbage with a click of a button.

It's fast & accessible because people already have their phone at all times. It relays less on companies or locations to implement on each garbage bin.
