The Problem

Discovery of the Problem

I was on campus and got frustrated that I couldn't tell exactly where my items fit into the bin. I expressed this as an 'example' of a problem we could explore to my teammates

Criteria for Product

Motivation

Project Goal: A product that is easily accessible to users that not only sorts garbage into the correct bin but also educates and motivates them to maintain this habit. In addition, to being adoptable in many different settings and for other businesses.

Target Audience: 13 - 40. Tech friendly. May not necessarily be actively eco-friendly but also attempt to do their part

The Solution

Solution 1

Attachable Device on Bins using machince learning & Physical Computing

Solution 2

Web App using machine learning to sort, user's location, QR code to activate & gamification
