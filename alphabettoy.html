Description & Concept

A playful & colorful responsive web application for children learning English and English letters, with easy direction by using color, animation and a linear path to follow. Thus, the focus was on typography, capturing the child's attention and giving the ability to navigate through the web app without understanding any form of English. After collecting inspiration & color, I chose a hot air balloon with the goal of it reaching the bottom.

For raw thought process, click here

Typography

Quicksand

After looking into usability studies, size 14 font was optimal. In addition, the best font for a children to see while they are learning, is the font that resembles how children are taught to write letters. I choose 'Quicksand', a sans-serif font. The only letter that was a problem was 'Q' (because it doesn't resemble hand writing), so I specifically only changed the Q to 'raleway'

Challenges

Besides learning how to make it responsive on my own accord. The main challenges was discovering what was the best method for a child to learn letters and sound, and prompting them to click what needed to be clicked.

Solutions
