United Way: Volunteer Portal

Proposed an additonal Volunteer Portal & marketing recommednation on website for United Way
Halton & Hamilton, to encourage young adults to take part by making it accesiable, easy & understand benefits

View Mockup

Skills Used: Project Managing, Presentation, navigation, UI & UX design


  • PROCESS

Description & Concept

We prototyped a 'volunteer portal' as an extension of the current website to make it feasible by building on top of what they currently have. The volunteer portal is a meant to make volunteering options more accessible to young adults, catered to the skill and education of the user if they create their own profile. The profile will contain information of them, so that recruit can be simpler.

Nuvo Hackathon Sponsored by United way of Halton & Hamilton

Solutions

1. Re-advertising, and listing volunteering board along with post-secondary job boards
Young adults concerns are their debt, possible career, and finding time to be social. Some education programs require volunteering, and there are volunteer positions are related to the field. If it's more widely seen to these student, there would be a much higher chance. We are focusing on young adults who are still completing there education because starting younger may motivate them because they will already be included in the community. .

My Role & Teammates

My role was as a UI/UX Designer. However, I also coded the front-end aspect, worked on the presentation portion, presented along side with my teammates & did Q&A. My teammates were a business analyst, data scientist and a developer. Because of my education in interaction design, I learned how to communicate between the three different fields despite knowing only small portions. We worked successfully and collaboratively in each portion of the work.

Challange

Finding what would motivate young adults to volunteering (18 - 30) age range. Keeping Young adults follow a habit of volunteering for the community. Making the solution feasible. Bringing awareness of the benefits of volunteering. A solution for making the outcome accessible. Ensure that the outcome follows the same branding by united way.


2. A portal on GenNext United Way for young adults to browse, engage, and apply to volunteer opportunities that suits their skill sets
Creating an extension of the original sites, would keep whomever is consistently visiting. When logging in, they may state their education, volunteer experience, and further on what they prefer to look for. In addition, remind them they need a background screening for certain position.

UI & UX

We followed United Way branding guidelines posted online, while also staying true to the current webpage. We color-coded each volunteer position based on their three pillars: health, education & financial stability. We focused on making the navigation familiar, trying to take example for job applying sites.
