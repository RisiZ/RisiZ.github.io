Our Solution

Club charity for water brigades trips. A website for visitors to browse, apply, plan and contact other team members. Focusing on UX & UI design



Why this Solution?

  • • The users are a student, club member, leader and someone who is looking to donate
  • • They need a platform where they can view all notifications, messages, and lists
  • • Want more members, and need the visitor to understand their goal

Features

  • Message

    Contact other team members through the messenger. The messages will be forwarded to the users email as well.

  • Notfication

    The user can view notifications on their profile, and keep track of all notifications from brigades they have joined, or applying for.

  • Planning

    A calender was suggested in the brief in the interface itself. However, because there's not many events occuring, we believed giving the user the ability to add it to their google calender or such is much more efficient.
